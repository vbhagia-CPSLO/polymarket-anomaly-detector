⚙️ How It Works

Every hour, the system polls Polymarket, filters to political/economic markets via LLM, runs a 5-signal anomaly engine, and classifies flagged trades.

Step 1
Fetch Markets
Top 100 by 24hr volume from Polymarket Gamma API
→
Step 2
LLM Filter
qwen2.5:14b classifies politics/economics relevance
→
Step 3
Fetch Trades
All trades from last hour for relevant markets
→
Step 4
Signal Engine
5 anomaly signals, flag if ≥2 triggered
→
Step 5
LLM Classify
Categorize as informed, wash, liquidity, or normal

Anomaly Signals

large_position
Trade ≥ $5,000
contrarian_trade
Price deviates >15pp from consensus (volatility-adjusted)
rapid_repeat_trades
Same wallet ≥3 trades in window
size_outlier
Trade > mean + 2σ for market
volume_price_shift
Price moved >10pp on <10% liquidity (volatility-adjusted)
